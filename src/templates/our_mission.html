about us: our mission

We're dedicated to making food more accessible. For low-income families and communities, financial hardship may hinder access to basic necessities. 

On the other hand, food goes to waste every day: from "imperfect" yet perfectly good produce in grocery stores to an incredible amount of extra food from college dining halls, food is thrown out without a second thought.

We hope that by creating a way to connect people with distributors and venues with surplus food, we can help to provide aid to those in need while redirecting food that would otherwise go to waste.

how you can help:

The more people know about our site, the more users it can help. Whenever you share to family, friends, restaurants, bakeries, grocery stores, and more, it helps to extend the reach of our site. We work together—consumers, distributors, and food donors—to tackle the issue of hunger and food waste in what ways we can. If our mission inspires you, please share our site to anyone you'd can. And if you are a part of a 501(c)(3) non-profit interested in helping our efforts to redistribute food, please consider registering with our site to become a distributor today!
